Post-Ride Stretching (15–20 min · Priority Order)

Minimum 3–4 days/week post-ride. Optimal: daily. Hip flexors = #1 priority.

Lower Body Priority
Kneeling Hip Flexor Lunge★★★★★
Hip flexors (psoas, iliacus, rectus femoris)
45–60s/side · 2 sets · Post-ride
Rear knee on ground, front foot forward. TUCK tailbone under. Arm overhead, lean away from stretch.
Quad Stretch (Standing)★★★★★
Quadriceps, rectus femoris
30–45s/side · 2 sets · Post-ride
Pull heel to glute. Squeeze glute of stretched leg. Add slight forward lean for deeper stretch.
Hamstring Stretch (Supine)★★★★★
Hamstrings, proximal calf
45–60s/side · 2 sets · Post-ride
Lie on back. Pull opposite leg toward chest. Keep leg as straight as possible.
Pigeon Pose / Figure-4★★★★☆
Glutes, piriformis, hip external rotators
60–90s/side · 1–2 sets · Post-ride/evening
Front leg at 90°, back leg extended. Fold forward for deeper stretch.
IT Band Foam Roll + Stretch★★★★☆
IT band, lateral quad, TFL
60–90s/side roll · Post-ride
Foam roll lateral thigh slowly. Cross-leg standing stretch: lean to opposite side.
Upper Body & Spine
Thoracic Extension Over Roller★★★★☆
Thoracic spine, chest, pec minor
60–90s at 3–4 levels · Post-ride/morning
Roller perpendicular across mid-back. Arms overhead. Let gravity extend thoracic spine.
Doorway Chest Stretch★★★★☆
Pectorals, anterior deltoid
30–45s/side · 2 sets · Any time
Arm at 90°, press elbow into doorway, rotate body away.
Calf Stretch (Gastroc + Soleus)★★★☆☆
Gastrocnemius, soleus, achilles
45–60s/side each position · Post-ride
Straight-leg wall stretch (gastroc) + bent-knee wall stretch (soleus). Both needed.
Child's Pose with Side Reach★★★☆☆
Lats, thoracic spine, hip flexors
30–45s each side · 2 sets · Any time
On knees, reach arms forward. Walk hands right/left for lat stretch on each side.

Hip flexors, glutes, adductors
15 swings each direction per leg · Pre-ride
